Oxygen Availability

We have an adequate number of oxygen cylinders in our stores. Also during these covid situation we are in touch with various other private oxygen suppliers to deal in the situation of emergency.

Ventilators

We have a large number of ventilators with proper treatment equipments. In these Covid sitautions we are constantly incresing number of ventilators in our hospital. Very soon we will be able to treat more number of patients.

Medicines

We provide a wide range of medicines availabiity in our medical stores.We have a proper stock of medicines used in the treatment of Covid 19. In these covid situation we are providing medicines at a highly subsidised rate to ensure that everyone can afford it.

Vaccines Availability

With the help Of Ministry of Healthcare ,India we are providing vaccine to every eligible person.For this you need to register through our portal or you can visit our primary healthcare department to get vaccinated.
